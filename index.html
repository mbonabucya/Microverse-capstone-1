Commonwealth Heads of Government Meeting 2022 (CHOGM)

Leaders of Commonwealth countries meet every two years for the Commonwealth Heads of Government Meeting (CHOGM), hosted by different member countries on a rotating basis. Since 1971, a total of 24 meetings have been held,the current meeting is held at Kigali, Rwanda.
